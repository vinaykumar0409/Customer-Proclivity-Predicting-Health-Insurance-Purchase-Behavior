Predicting Health Insurance Purchase Behavior

About Health Insurance Cross-Sell Prediction

In the competitive insurance market, acquiring new customers is significantly more expensive than retaining existing ones. Therefore, cross-selling additional insurance products to existing customers is a crucial strategy for increasing revenue. This project aims to develop a predictive model that identifies which customers are likely to purchase additional health insurance coverage.

Given the historical data on customer demographics, vehicle attributes, and previous insurance details, we aim to predict the likelihood of a customer purchasing health insurance. By accurately predicting this likelihood, the insurance company can effectively target their marketing efforts and improve their cross-selling strategy.
